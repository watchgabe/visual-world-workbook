Workshop 1

Your Funnel

Most creators think they have an audience problem. They think they need more followers. But the real problem is they have no system to capture the attention they’re already generating. This module fixes that.

Once it’s built, it runs automatically. You create content. Content drives people into the funnel. The funnel converts them into buyers. You wake up to sales.

The Five Stages

01
Content — Awareness
Every piece of content has one job: drive people to the next stage. Your CTA should point to your lead magnet. Not your offer. Not your website. Your lead magnet. Asking someone who just discovered you to buy something is like proposing on a first date.
02
Lead Magnet — Capture
A free resource that delivers one specific outcome in exchange for an email address. This is where you convert a follower (who you don’t own) into an email subscriber (who you do). Email has 40x higher ROI than social media and converts at 3–5x the rate.
03
Newsletter — Relationship
Not a promotional email. Pure value delivery. Every newsletter should make your subscriber feel like they’re getting more than they paid for. Give so much value that people feel guilty not buying from you.
04
Offer — Conversion
After someone has consumed your content, downloaded your lead magnet, and read your newsletter — they trust you. Now you can make an offer. By this stage, the sale should feel obvious.
05
Customer — Retention
The sale is not the end. A customer who gets results becomes a testimonial. A testimonial becomes social proof. Social proof drives more people into the top of your funnel. Deliver so much value that your customers become your best marketers.

ManyChat & Newsletter

ManyChat and email are the two most powerful platforms for automating your funnel. Together, they turn your content into a 24/7 lead generation machine — converting followers into subscribers and subscribers into buyers, without you manually following up with anyone.

ManyChat — Comment Triggers

ManyChat connects to your Instagram (and Facebook) and watches for specific keywords in your comments. When someone comments your trigger word — like “GUIDE” or “FREE” — ManyChat instantly fires an automated DM to that person.

That DM contains your lead magnet link. They click it, enter their email, and they’re added to your list. Comment-triggered DMs convert at 70–75% — versus 50–55% for a standard landing page. They feel personal, keep people inside the app, and capture leads whether you’re awake or asleep.

How the Flow Works

01
You post with a keyword CTA
Your caption ends with: “Comment GUIDE and I’ll DM it to you.” This single line turns every post into an active lead generator. The keyword can be anything — a word, a number, even a phrase like “SAUSAGES” if that fits the bit.
02
ManyChat detects the keyword
The moment someone comments your keyword, ManyChat fires automatically. It posts a short public reply to their comment and sends them a DM — no manual work, no missed leads, no delays.
03
DM delivers the lead magnet
The DM contains your lead magnet link with a tap-to-get button. They click, land on a simple form, and enter their email. At this point they’re in your system and no longer just a follower you don’t own.
04
Email captured in Kit or your platform
ManyChat connects directly to Kit (formerly ConvertKit), Flodesk, Beehiiv, and most major email platforms. The moment someone opts in, their email is automatically added to your list and your welcome sequence fires immediately.
05
Welcome sequence runs automatically
A 7–10 email sequence delivers your best content, tells your story, builds real trust, and introduces your offer — all on autopilot over the first two weeks. You set it up once. It runs forever.

The Welcome Sequence

Your welcome sequence is the most read email series you will ever send. Open rates run 50–60% higher than regular newsletters — because people just opted in and they’re at peak interest. This is the window where trust is built fastest. Use it.

Email 1 (Immediately): Deliver the lead magnet. Confirm what they signed up for. Be warm and direct — not salesy.

Email 2 (Day 2): Your origin story. The relatable struggle that brought you here. Make them feel like they know you.

Email 3 (Day 4): Your single best free teaching piece — a framework or insight so good they can’t believe it’s free.

Email 4 (Day 7): A client or student result. Social proof. A soft, natural mention of your offer.

Emails 5–10 (optional): Additional frameworks, case studies, objection handling, or a direct offer sequence — based on how aggressive you want your funnel to be.

Why These Two Platforms

Social media is rented land. Every follower you have lives on a platform that can change its algorithm, ban your account, or disappear entirely. ManyChat converts that rented attention into owned contacts. Email is where the relationship deepens into real trust — and real trust is what drives sales. Together, ManyChat and email are the infrastructure that makes your content work for you long after you’ve posted it.

Bio Optimization

Your profile is the first thing people see when your content lands in front of them. It has about two seconds to answer one question: “Is this person for me?” There are five elements that determine whether they follow, click, or leave. Every one of them matters.

#1 — Link in Bio

The old approach was a Linktree with multiple links. The new approach is a single link that directs viewers exactly where you want them to go. Multiple options create friction. One clear destination creates action.

Your link should point directly to your lead magnet landing page — not your website, not a menu of options. The goal of every piece of content is to move someone from follower to email subscriber. Make that the only door.

Your Link in Bio
My single link (lead magnet landing page)

#2 — Username

The old approach was a random handle. The new approach is either your real name (if it’s available) or your exact business name. Your username is searchable — make it work for you.

Random handles with underscores, numbers, or clever wordplay are harder to find and harder to remember. If your name is taken, add your niche after it: @yourname.brand or @yournamecreates.

Your Username
My handle (across platforms)

#3 — Tagline (Name Field)

The old approach was just your name in the name field. The new approach is your name followed by your occupation, a niche keyword, or a 1–2 word answer that clearly communicates what you do. This field is searchable — treat it like a headline, not a label.

Example: Gabe | Cinematics | Personal Branding

Your Tagline (Instagram / TikTok Name Field)
Name field tagline

#4 — Profile Picture

Your profile picture is displayed at thumbnail size. It needs to work at 50px. That means: close-up face, single person, high contrast background, professional quality. Far-away shots, group photos, and blurry images lose immediately at small sizes.

Your face is your brand. People follow people. A clean, clear headshot builds more trust in a thumbnail than any logo ever will.

#5 — The Bio

The old approach was messy with no structure. The new approach follows a simple 4-line structure that answers every question a new visitor has before they even have to ask it.

Line 1: What do you do/sell and who you help

Line 2: How do people work with you / buy from you

Line 3: Some sort of credibility

Line 4: CTA

Launch Content

Three pinned pieces of content that work 24/7. Every new visitor lands on your profile and immediately knows who you are, what you stand for, and why they should follow you. Build these once. Let them run forever.

Format is flexible. These don’t have to be videos — a reel, a carousel, or a long-form post all work. The medium is secondary. The message is everything. Pin all three and let them do the selling while you sleep.

Post #1 — Your Story

This is the single best use of real estate on your profile. Every person who lands on your page will see this first — it gives them an immediate look into who you are, what you do, and why they might want to stick around. This is not a highlight reel. This is the honest version: the real journey, the real turning point, the real reason you show up. A new visitor should finish this and feel like they already know you.

Post #2 — Your Positioning Deep Dive

This is your flag in the ground. You’re not just introducing yourself — you’re teaching the subject you want to be known for through your beliefs. This video tells the world exactly where you stand, what you believe that others don’t say out loud, and why your POV matters. It’s not broad education. It’s how you see the space differently. This is what separates you from every other creator in your niche.

Post #3 — The Authority Masterclass

This is a 30+ minute long-form video that covers everything you know about your subject — a complete, comprehensive resource that someone could watch and immediately understand why they should trust you. This is not bragging. This is proof. Someone who has never heard of you will finish this and think: this person knows exactly what they’re talking about. The best part? This single video becomes months of short-form content through your waterfall system. Build it once. Repurpose it forever.
