Workshop 1

Automated Sales Funnel

Most creators think they have an audience problem. They think they need more followers. But the real problem is they have no system to capture the attention they’re already generating. This module fixes that.

Once it’s built, it runs automatically. You create content. Content drives people into the funnel. The funnel converts them into buyers. You wake up to sales.

The Five Stages

01
Content — Awareness
Every piece of content has one job: drive people to the next stage. Your CTA should point to your lead magnet. Not your offer. Not your website. Your lead magnet. Asking someone who just discovered you to buy something is like proposing on a first date.
02
Lead Magnet — Capture
A free resource that delivers one specific outcome in exchange for an email address. This is where you convert a follower (who you don’t own) into an email subscriber (who you do). Email has 40x higher ROI than social media and converts at 3–5x the rate.
03
Newsletter — Relationship
Not a promotional email. Pure value delivery. Every newsletter should make your subscriber feel like they’re getting more than they paid for. Give so much value that people feel guilty not buying from you.
04
Offer — Conversion
After someone has consumed your content, downloaded your lead magnet, and read your newsletter — they trust you. Now you can make an offer. By this stage, the sale should feel obvious.
05
Customer — Retention
The sale is not the end. A customer who gets results becomes a testimonial. A testimonial becomes social proof. Social proof drives more people into the top of your funnel. Deliver so much value that your customers become your best marketers.

ManyChat + Newsletter

ManyChat turns your Instagram comments into an automated lead machine. When someone comments a keyword, ManyChat sends them a DM with your lead magnet automatically — no manual work, no missed leads. Combine this with your newsletter and you have an audience you truly own.

Comment-triggered DMs convert at 70–75% vs 50–55% for a landing page. It drives engagement, feels personal, and captures leads without requiring people to leave the app.

Your lead magnet is the bridge between your social media audience and your email list. It’s a free resource that delivers one specific outcome in exchange for an email address. It sets the tone for everything that follows.

A great lead magnet does three things: attracts the right people, delivers a quick win, and creates desire for more. Your lead magnet should deliver ONE specific outcome in 10–20 minutes.

Workshop 4

Bio Optimization

Your bio is the most underrated piece of real estate in your entire brand. It’s your positioning statement, your value proposition, and your CTA — all in 150 characters or less. Most creators waste it. A great bio does three things in three seconds: tells them who you help, what you help them do, and what to do next.

The Bio Formula

Line 1: “I help [avatar] [achieve specific outcome]”

Line 2: “Through [your method/approach]”

Line 3: Social proof or credibility — a specific result or credential

Line 4: “[Clear action] → [Link]”

Build Your Bio
Line 1 — Who you help + what you help them do
Line 2 — How you do it + what makes you different
Line 3 — Social proof or credibility
Line 4 — CTA
